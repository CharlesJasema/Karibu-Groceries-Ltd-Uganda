How KGL Branch Access Works

Step 1 – Open Landing Page

Managers and sales agents always start from this landing page, where they choose their branch (Maganjo or Matugga) and role.

Step 2 – Secure Login

After selecting a branch and role, the system guides you to the KGL login page where your username and password are verified using secure JWT authentication.

Step 3 – Role‑Based Dashboards

Successful logins are routed straight to the correct dashboard: Manager, Sales Agent, or Director, with permissions tailored to your responsibilities.
